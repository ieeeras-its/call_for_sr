TC on Autonomous Ground Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems’s call for Student Representatives

Overview

Dear Robo-Colleagues,

The IEEE Robotics & Automation Society (RAS) is excited to announce an open position for a "Student Representative” to support the activities of the Autonomous Ground Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems Autonomous Ground Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems .

Role Description

As a Student Representative, you will work closely with the Chair and Co-Chairs of the Technical Committee on Autonomous Ground Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems, assisting in the organization and coordination of technical activities.

Your responsibilities will include:

  1. Assisting in the planning and execution of committee events and initiatives (e.g., meetings, workshops etc.).
  2. Co-Managing the Twitter and LinkedIn pages of the TC.
  3. Promoting the committee's work and recruiting new student members.
  4. Representing the student perspective within the committee.

Requirements

  1. Current enrollment in a Ph.D. or M.S.-Ph.D. program related to autonomous ground vehicles and intelligent transportation systems.
  2. Demonstrated interest in the robotics and ITS fields.
  3. Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  4. Ability to commit to the role and volunteer for 2-3 years.

Benefits

As a Student Representative, you will work closely with the Chair and Co-Chairs of the Technical Committee on Autonomous Ground Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems, assisting in the organization and coordination of technical activities.

Your responsibilities will include:

  1. Opportunity to network with professionals and researchers in the robotics and ITS fields..
  2. Gain leadership experience and contribute to the advancement of the field of robotics and ITS.
  3. Support RAS flagship conferences to help with events and workshops organization.
  4. Advance your understanding of the field of robotics and ITS, and learn about ongoing work across multiple communities.

Application

If you meet the requirements and are excited about the opportunity to contribute to the community, we encourage you to apply.

By March 24, 2025, please submit to the link: https://forms.gle/jvpDLQb7ADyokQ1WA.

  1. your CV
  2. 1-page letter of interest
  3. 1-page letter of support from your supervisor (a few sentences expressing support are enough)

We look forward to your application!
